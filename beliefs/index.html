Our Core Beliefs

  • We believe God the Father, Son and Holy Spirit are all working together to offer every human being forgiveness of sins, a restored relationship and the power to live a victorious Christian life through the gift of the indwelling Holy Spirit.
  • We accept that Jesus Christ loves each one of us so much that He was willing to come and die in our place, taking the penalty that we deserve and giving us the gift of His life. If we personally accept God’s gift of salvation, we here and now become members of God’s family.
  • We look forward to the literal second coming of Jesus Christ and believe that the Bible warns us what this world will be like just prior to that event. As we look around today we see that the conditions in our world indicate that it may not be long before Jesus returns to finally sort out the mess that humans have made of a once beautiful creation.
  • The Bible is our guide for daily living and we practice the Ten Commandments, not from a sense of obligation or to enable an individual to “earn” a place in Heaven, but rather, having a daily relationship with God, we love Him and want to follow His best plan for our lives.
  • Jesus invites us into His family and we can simply let Him know that we happily accept His offer by being baptized. Baptism is a symbol of our old life being washed away and our coming up out of the water to a new life filled with God’s free gifts of grace, forgiveness, righteousness and freedom. We believe that eternal life starts the moment we welcome Jesus into our lives as Lord and Saviour.
  • We meet together each Saturday (Sabbath) because we love our Creator and Redeemer and this day reminds us who has made us and saved us. It is the day God chose Himself. In Jesus we find our true rest, joy and completeness, and we hope that this will be your experience too.
